a&d for left and right.
space bar for jump.
left click a block to break it.
right click a block to place it under you.
(Some blocks can not be placed or broken.)
tab for hotbar (does nothing yet.)
S to save current game.
enter to load saved game.
r to reset world.
escape to quit game.
f11 for fullscreen.
p for rain.
o for snow.
num 2 for beach area.
